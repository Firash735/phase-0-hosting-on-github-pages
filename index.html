Top Vacation Spots

By: Abdisharif Abdi
Published: Today

The world is full of fascinating places. Planning the perfect vacation involves packing up, leaving home, and experiencing something new.

1. Maasai Mara National Reserve

One of the most spectacular wildlife events in the world. Over 1.5 million wildebeest, along with hundreds of thousands of zebras and gazelles, migrate from the Serengeti to the Maasai Mara in search of greener pastures. Learn More.
Top Attractions
  • The Great Migration
  • Big Five Game Viewing
  • Hot Air Balloon Safaris

2. Malindi and Watamu

Malindi is a historic coastal town located in Kenya's Kilifi County. It is known for its rich Swahili culture, beautiful beaches, and historical significance.Watamu is a small coastal town located south of Malindi. It is famous for its stunning beaches, coral reefs, and vibrant marine life. Learn More.
Top Attractions
  • Vasco da Gama Pillar
  • Gedi Ruins
  • Watamu Beach

3. Tsavo National Parks (East and West)

Tsavo National Park, located in southeastern Kenya, is one of the largest national parks in the world, divided into two distinct parts: Tsavo East and Tsavo West. Together, they cover an area of approximately 22,000 square kilometers. The park is known for its diverse landscapes, abundant wildlife, and rich history. Learn More.
Top Attractions
  • Mzima Springs
  • Ngulia Rhino Sanctuary
  • Lake Jipe

More Destinations
